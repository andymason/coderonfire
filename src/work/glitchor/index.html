Glitchor

Glitching or Databending is about manipulating data to introduce artifices that result in distortions from the original source. To explore data-bending more I created Glitchor, a tool that let me create my own glitched images.

By directly manipulating the RGB data-structure of a source image I am able to generate unique artistic results.
